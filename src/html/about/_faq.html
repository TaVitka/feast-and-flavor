Frequently Asked Question

  • What types of events does Feast and Flavor cater to?

    Feast and Flavor caters to a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate functions, private parties, and special occasions. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a large-scale event, we provide customized menus and exceptional service to meet your needs.

  • Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or special requests?

    Absolutely! At Feast and Flavor, we understand the importance of catering to diverse dietary needs and preferences. Our culinary team is skilled at accommodating various dietary restrictions, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more. We're also happy to incorporate any special requests into your menu.

  • How far in advance should I book catering services for my event?

    We recommend booking catering services as early as possible to ensure availability for your desired date. Ideally, we suggest booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for peak wedding and event seasons. However, we understand that sometimes plans change, so don't hesitate to reach out to us even if your event is coming up soon.

  • What is the process for arranging a tasting or consultation?

    To arrange a tasting or consultation, simply contact us via email to schedule an appointment, or fill out booking form. During the consultation, our team will discuss your event details, preferences, and any specific requirements you may have. We'll also provide samples of our culinary offerings for you to taste and discuss menu options tailored to your event.

  • How does pricing for catering services work?

    Our pricing for catering services is based on several factors, including the type of event, menu selections, guest count, and any additional services or enhancements requested. We strive to provide transparent pricing and will work with you to create a customized package that fits your budget. For detailed pricing information and a personalized quote, please reach out to us directly.
