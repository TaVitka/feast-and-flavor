Evolution of the company's services

  • 2010

    The Beginning

    Started with bespoke dinner parties and small gatherings, focusing on quality and personal touch.

  • 2013

    Corporate Events Expansion

    Expanded our services to include corporate events, offering tailored menus and professional service for business functions of all sizes.

  • 2016

    Weddings and Large-scale Events

    Launched our wedding catering service, quickly becoming a sought-after provider for couples looking for a personalized dining experience on their special day.

  • 2020

    Sustainable Catering Initiatives

    Introduced eco-friendly practices across our operations, from sourcing to serving, emphasizing our commitment to the environment and sustainable gastronomy.
